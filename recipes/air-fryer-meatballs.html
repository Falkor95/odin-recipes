Air Fryer Meatballs!

After trying air fryer meatballs, I will never bake them in the oven again. The insides stay nice and tender while the outsides get a slight crisp on them. The best part is that you can make them ahead of time and freeze for later use. I have included those directions in the notes.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Egg
  • Seasoning
